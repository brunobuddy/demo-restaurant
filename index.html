Welcome to Our Inn

Nestled in the heart of the majestic Vercors mountains, The Summit Inn welcomes you in a warm and authentic setting. For three generations, we have perpetuated the mountain culinary tradition with passion and respect for local products.

Our traditional inn offers breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks, and our crackling fireplace will warm you up after your mountain hikes.
